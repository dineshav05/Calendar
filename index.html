Why Observe Ekadashi?

Scientific Benefit: Autophagy

Fasting for 24 hours gives your digestive system a complete rest. This triggers Autophagy, a biological process where cells clean out damaged components and toxins. It lowers insulin levels and reduces inflammation.

Spiritual Science: The Lunar Pull

Just as the moon moves the oceans, it affects the water in the human body. On the 11th day (Ekadashi), the atmospheric pressure is lowest. Fasting on this day prevents fluid retention and utilizes this natural energy updraft to control the mind and senses.
